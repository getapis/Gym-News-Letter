💡 This Week's Tip

Rest Is Part of the Work

You don't build strength during training — you build it during recovery. If you've been showing up every single day without a rest day, your body is likely running on stress hormones, not adaptation. Aim for at least one full rest day per week and prioritise 7–9 hours of sleep. Recovery isn't a reward for hard work. It is the work.
