💡 This Week's Tip
REST IS NOT WEAKNESS.

You don't build strength during training — you build it during recovery. If you've been grinding every single day, your body is running on stress hormones, not adaptation. Aim for at least one full rest day this week and protect your sleep like it's a training session. Because it is. Recovery isn't a reward. It's the strategy.
